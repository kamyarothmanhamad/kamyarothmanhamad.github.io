    My Research in Simpler Terms

    In simpler terms, I'm exploring how to teach computers to "see" and make sense of 3D objects. Imagine a pile of dots that represents the shape and surface of a chair. That's a 3D point cloud! My job is to develop clever algorithms using deep learning (a type of artificial intelligence) to analyze these point clouds and help computers understand the 3D world.
